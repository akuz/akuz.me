Software / VSound App

VSound is a real-time audio visualization app available on macOS and iOS, designed to transform sound into captivating visuals. Built using C++, Swift, and Metal, it leverages GPU acceleration for seamless performance.

Unlike traditional FFT-based visualizers, VSound employs a novel frequency detection algorithm that automatically adapts to the audio input, providing high-precision visual feedback without manual configuration. This makes it an excellent tool for:

  • Creating music visualizations for videos
  • Enhancing the listening experience with dynamic visuals
  • Practicing singing or learning tonal languages by visualizing pitch and tone
  • Assisting individuals with hearing impairments to perceive sound frequencies visually
