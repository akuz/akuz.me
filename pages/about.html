About

This is a personal website of Andrey Kuzmenko.

I am an applied mathematician, software engineer, quantitative analyst, experienced with- and interested in Machine Learning (ML), Large Language Models (LLMs), other Transformer based methods, Deep Neural Networks (Conv, LSTM, etc, which have now largely been all replaced with Transformer based architectures anyway), Probabilistic Graphical Models (PGM), Bayesian Inference and Learning. I am also a confident user of linear algebra and optimisation methods. I currently actively code in Python/C++ and Swift/Metal, but also have a lot of experience with Java, JavaScript, C, C#, SQL. I can quickly learn to code in any programming language (during my long life I’ve even programmed in Basic, Perl, Pascal/Delphi, ASP, and other long forgotten languages). My favourite application areas are natural language processing (NLP), computer vision (CV), quantitative finance (QF) and, a more recent personal challenge, electronic music generation using deep learning (only shallowly explored due to time constraints).

Opinions expressed here do not represent those of my current or past employers.
